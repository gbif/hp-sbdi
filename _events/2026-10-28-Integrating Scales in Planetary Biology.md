As environmental change reshapes ecosystems worldwide, understanding how biological mechanisms — from cells to organisms to ecosystems — enable adaptation and resilience has become an urgent research challenge.

**The Planetary Biology Strategic Area is thrilled to announce this conference designed to foster cross-disciplinary collaboration, connecting scientists working across scales with industry, funders, and policy-makers who can translate insights into practice.**

Highlights of the programme include:
- Scientific talks across disciplines
- Round table discussions
- Panels with funders and policy-makers
