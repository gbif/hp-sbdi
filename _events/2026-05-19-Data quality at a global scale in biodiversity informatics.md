His focus: **data quality at a global scale in biodiversity informatics**.

Global biodiversity databases are critical for research and conservation, but ensuring their quality is a growing challenge. How do we manage errors, inconsistencies, and uncertainty across millions of records from diverse sources?

Dr Westgate will explore how we can strengthen data systems by embedding **transparency, peer review, and explicit treatment of uncertainty** directly into biodiversity infrastructures.
This is key to **building trust in data, improving decisions, and enabling effective global collaboration**.
