 The 7th edition of the World Conference on Marine Biodiversity will be organized in Bruges between November 17th and 20th 2026. 
---

The WCMB 2026 will fly the [UN Ocean Decade](https://oceandecade.org/) flag, as it has been endorsed as a UN Ocean Decade Event. The overall theme of the Conference is ***“The marine biodiversity insights we need, for the ocean we want”***. As the Conference will take place a little over halfway through the UN Ocean Decade, it will provide an excellent opportunity to take stock of ongoing actions and initiatives, but also evaluate what still needs to be tackled by the end of the Decade.
