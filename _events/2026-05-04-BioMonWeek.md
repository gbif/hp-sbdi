This inaugural edition marks a launch guided by a vision to deliver an impactful biannual format. The event will bring together science, policy, and business communities to exchange monitoring knowledge, build networks, and drive tangible societal impact to help reverse the global biodiversity crisis.

By building a welcoming space for the biodiversity monitoring community, it will foster a common culture and understanding of biodiversity monitoring in Europe, and advance transnational and cross-sector cooperation.
